Invoice Week #1

  

Date & Time: 6.13.2026, 12:18 PM

Less than a month ago, I got admitted to the SPARK A2 program at the digital clinic. That was exciting, considering I have been constantly applying for jobs. In my interview, I shared that I am seeking a role where I can gain hands-on experience in graphic design and UX/UI. I also mentioned that I would like to use my new skills to help people. When SPARK A2 contacted me back and told me that I got the position, I was very happy. I felt valuable. When they matched me with the startup named VoiceBlocks, I was even happier to be in the program with Dr. Lonna Hardin. VoiceBlocks is a startup company whose experts are in the K-12 IEP process. In the onboarding week, I learned about the IEP process. It is interesting to see the gap between what parents should know and what they truly know, and this gap impacts the child. I was surprised to discover that teachers and education systems are also struggling with meeting expectations in confirming that the child with needs will receive a personalized IEP program. Crystal and I pair with Voiceblocks, and we are both motivated to contribute to the company. It seems that Crystal will focus on research and analysis, and I will focus on design. The collaboration between our responsibilities will create a better design for the user experience. It seems that we collaborate well with each other. My first project is focused on creating content for marketing purposes. I used VoiceBlocks' old materials and refreshed them. In the process of creating short new videos, I made intro and outro videos using the VoiceBlocks logo. It feels to me like a valuable contribution. For the following week, I plan to create a whole video with IEP content. And if it works smoothly, I’ll be able to create some more of these IEP videos quickly. For the next week, I will also dedicate some of my marketing training time. I feel grateful for this opportunity.

  

Invoice Week #2

  

Date & Time: 6.20.2026, 2:40 PM

This week was full of surprises. At Spark, the mother company that hired me, I began my Google Analytics certification. I’m making some progress - I completed the first 12 activities. It’s about a fifth of the certification studies. Roughly, I learned about the purpose of analyzing users’ habits to optimize the organization’s marketing. Dr. Lonna, the incubator company’s CEO, plays a meaningful role in my life as a mentor. She does her best to push me to achieve better results. Valuables: I made some media assets using PS and Canva. I even posted some of them on social media using Buffer. Currently, I am working on a new project: a series of explanatory videos on special needs. Our CEO aims to make this series part of the community's knowledge base that the company has been building so far. I need to accomplish 13 scripts and create 13 videos. Values: This week, I learned to pay closer attention to the instructions from my supervisor. I also learned to ask questions that help me create content that meets the company's satisfaction. I learned how to map the entities involved in a business, the audience with the market gap, and the stakeholders with the solutions. I am grateful for the opportunity that came my way and look forward to the next.
